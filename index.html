Training Tips

Train with app at least 3X per week.
For best results 3 times per week is the recommended training frequency. Training once a day is even better!

Start Training

Train when you're alert and not tired.
The more alert you are during your training session, the better it is. Avoid training when you are fatigued.

Start Training

Train under dim lighting condition.
It is not necessary for the room to be pitch-black, just make sure there are no strong surrounding lights.

Start Training

Hold the device 15 in. (40cm) from your eyes.
Users frequently don't pay attention and train with the device too far from their eyes... watch out!
